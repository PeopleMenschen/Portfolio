    Electrical Distribution Boards for Single-Family Residential Buildings

    Electrical Distribution Boards for Single-Family Residential Buildings. This project involved the assembly and wiring of electrical distribution boards, specifically for single-family homes and small residential buildings. These boards were designed and installed to ensure safe, organized, and reliable power distribution in compliance with fundamental electrical principles and standards.

    My role in this project included the following tasks:
    Installation and layout of modular protective devices (miniature circuit breakers, surge protective devices (SPDs), residual current devices (RCDs/RCCBs), and earth leakage protection).
    Mounting of terminals and power distribution components. Application of basic electrical calculations, adherence to safety regulations, and wiring execution.

    Components Used in the Board:

    This distribution board serves as the main protective and distribution unit in single-family residential buildings and includes the following key components:
    - Residual Current Device (RCD / FI switch): Protects occupants against electric shock due to current leakage and enhances overall system safety.
    - Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs): Protect individual circuits against overload and short-circuit faults.
    - Surge Protective Device (SPD): Safeguards electrical equipment against transient overvoltages and voltage surges.
    - Phase, Neutral, and Earth (PE) Terminals: Enable organized and secure distribution of conductors within the enclosure. Auxiliary Control and Power Modules: Provide control voltage for auxiliary devices within the panel.

    Larger-Scale Electrical Distribution Board

    Larger-Scale Electrical Distribution Board. This is a slightly larger-scale electrical distribution board, designed for commercial, office, or multi-unit residential buildings. The panel is constructed in a modular configuration to allow for future expansion, clear circuit segregation, and ease of system upgrades.

    Technical features of this board include:
    - Preparation for multiple lighting and power consumer circuits
    - Use of protective and distribution devices across multiple levels
    - Logical circuit separation for enhanced safety and simplified maintenance. Organized and planned wiring to support smooth final project completion

    This project demonstrates my ability to contribute to electrical panels ranging from small to medium scale, adherence to standard structural practices, and professional preparation of distribution boards for final installation and commissioning phases.

    Electrical Panel Enclosure Frame / Skeleton

    The metal frame of the electrical distribution panel is designed for medium to large-scale applications and serves as the primary structural base for mounting electrical components in building or light industrial distribution boards. This frame provides the necessary mechanical structure to ensure organized, safe, and standardized installation of electrical equipment.

    Features and Applications:
    - Standardized metal DIN rails for mounting protective devices (e.g., circuit breakers, surge protectors), fuses, contactors, and control components
    - Copper busbars installed in the lower section for current distribution, as well as neutral (N) and protective earth (PE) connections
    - Suitable for main or sub-distribution boards in large residential buildings, office complexes, or semi-industrial installations
    - Proper preparation of this frame is a critical foundation for precise and code-compliant assembly of electrical components
